### What lives here

**[claude-cognitive-calibration](https://github.com/dallasandrews1/claude-cognitive-calibration)**
A pre-flight check that forces Claude to confront its known failure modes before generating output. Cuts down on slop. Useful if you've ever asked Claude for something and gotten something that looked confident and was wrong.

**[gtm-account-intel](https://github.com/dallasandrews1/gtm-account-intel)**
The research engine. Pulls earnings calls, regulatory filings, press releases, and leadership changes, then compresses everything into one Primary Forcing Function for a target account. Used for every Tier-1 account I work.

**[outreach-first-draft-engine](https://github.com/dallasandrews1/outreach-first-draft-engine)**
Forces prospect-first thinking before any outreach gets drafted. The first draft sounds like a person wrote it, not a template.

**[outreach-copy-sharpener](https://github.com/dallasandrews1/outreach-copy-sharpener)**
Takes the draft and sharpens it to send-ready C-suite quality. Applies the CTA Philosophy and the Tenbit++ framework. The output is something you can actually send to a CIO without flinching.
